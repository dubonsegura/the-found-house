Brand Kit

The Found House identity.

A visual system inspired by the first sculpture that helped shape the company.

The emblem

The Found House emblem is inspired by Continuum, the first sculptural piece that helped define the spirit of the brand. It suggests movement, discovery, craft, and the journey a remarkable object takes before finding its next home.

The mark should feel sculptural, quiet, timeless, and distinctive — never generic.

Identity system

  • Primary Logo: Abstract emblem with “The Found House.”
  • FH Monogram: For stickers, wax seals, social media, and small applications.
  • Found House Seal: For Story & Collection Records, packaging, and hang tags.
  • Shipping Mark: Simplified version for boxes, labels, and tape.
  • Watermark: Subtle mark for product photography and documents.

Working colors

  • Found House Ivory — #F6F4EE
  • Heritage Olive — #6B7057
  • Warm Taupe — #D6CBB7
  • Soft Charcoal — #222220
  • Antique Gold — #B89A5B, accent only
