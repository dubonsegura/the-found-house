Documentation

The Found House Collection Certificate.

An elegant record of the information known about a piece at the time it enters The Found House collection.

What it may include

  • Found House catalog number
  • Item title and category
  • Maker or artist, when known
  • Country of origin, when known
  • Materials and dimensions
  • Condition summary
  • Research notes and curator observations
  • Date added to The Found House collection

Important wording standard: A Found House Collection Certificate documents our research and observations. It is not an independent appraisal, valuation, or blanket guarantee of authenticity unless explicitly stated and supported by documentation.
