FAQ

Questions before launch.

Simple answers help customers feel comfortable.

Are your pieces new or old?

They may be vintage, artisan-made, gently used, beautifully aged, or occasionally newer. The common thread is that each piece is chosen with intention.

Do all items include a certificate?

Qualifying pieces may include a Found House Collection Certificate and Story Card. Some smaller or lower-documentation items may not.

Do you guarantee authenticity?

We document what we know and what our research supports. We avoid unsupported guarantees and clearly state uncertainty when it exists.

Who pays return shipping?

For approved discretionary returns, the customer pays return shipping. If The Found House made an error, we work to correct it fairly.

Do you work with designers?

Yes. We welcome interior designers, decorators, stagers, Airbnb hosts, hospitality professionals, and homeowners.
