The idea

Every remarkable piece deserves a remarkable home.

Beautiful things deserve to be discovered, appreciated, and enjoyed again. Some are handmade. Others are vintage. Some are contemporary. What connects them is not only age, but craftsmanship, character, and the quiet way they change a room.

Design worth discovering

Objects with character. Stories worth continuing.

Each piece is selected with care because it feels worthy of another chapter. The Found House is built for homeowners, collectors, designers, gift buyers, and anyone who believes a home should hold more than ordinary things.

A Found House signature

Every piece has a story.

Whenever possible, qualifying pieces include a Found House Story Card and Story & Collection Record with catalog details, research notes, condition summary, and curator observations.

We value honesty over exaggeration. When something is known, we say so. When something is uncertain, we say that too.

The Found House mark

Inspired by the first remarkable object.

The Found House emblem is inspired by the sculptural movement of Continuum, the first piece that helped shape the direction of the company. It reminds us that beautiful objects have journeys before they find their next home.
