Why The Found House Exists

The Found House began with a simple belief: beautiful things should not be forgotten simply because they have been replaced. Every object has a story. Our purpose is to discover remarkable pieces, learn what we can about them, and connect them with people who will appreciate living with them.

Remarkable things deserve remarkable homes.

“Every decision should make The Found House more trusted tomorrow than it is today.”
