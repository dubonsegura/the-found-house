Discover the Collection Why We Started

More than a store

A place for discovery.

The Found House searches for remarkable pieces — whether vintage, artisan-made, beautifully aged, or simply overlooked — and gives them the opportunity to begin a new chapter.

Some visitors come looking for a statement piece. Others are searching for something they did not even know they needed. Either way, welcome.

Why we started

Every remarkable piece deserves another chapter.

Beautiful things should not be forgotten simply because they have been replaced. Every object has a story. Some represent extraordinary craftsmanship. Others carry decades of memories. Many simply deserve another opportunity to be appreciated.

Our purpose is to discover these pieces, learn about them whenever possible, and thoughtfully connect them with people who will love living with them.

A Found House signature

Every piece has a story.

Whenever possible, qualifying pieces include a Found House Story Card and a Found House Story & Collection Record documenting catalog information, research notes, condition summary, and curator observations.

We value honesty over exaggeration. When something is known, we say so. When something is uncertain, we say that too.
