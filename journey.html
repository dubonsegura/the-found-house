Our Journey

It began with one remarkable object.

The Found House is being built carefully, one decision and one piece at a time.

The first mark of the story.

Every company has a beginning. For The Found House, the beginning was a sculptural piece known as Continuum — an object with movement, mystery, and presence.

That first sculpture helped shape the visual identity of the company. It reminded us that remarkable pieces often travel unexpected paths before finding the home where they truly belong.

The Found House emblem is inspired by that idea: movement, discovery, craftsmanship, and the journey from one chapter to the next.

The story continues with every piece.

As the collection grows, this page will quietly document the journey of The Found House: the first pieces, the first photographs, the first Story & Collection Records, the first customers, and the moments that shape the company.

The focus will always remain on the objects and the homes they enter. The story is not only ours. It belongs to every remarkable piece that finds its next chapter.
