Our Philosophy

Remarkable things deserve remarkable homes.

Found House Co. searches for furniture, art, décor, and one-of-a-kind pieces with character, craftsmanship, and story.

Every item is carefully chosen because we believe great design deserves another chapter.

Some pieces are preserved just as we found them. Others are thoughtfully restored while honoring their history.

Our hope is simple: to connect exceptional pieces with people who will appreciate them for years to come.

We are in the discovery business.

Every piece earns its place.
