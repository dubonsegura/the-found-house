Our Philosophy

Collected with Intention.
Meant to Be Lived With.

The Found House began with a simple belief: the objects we choose to live with shape the feeling of a home.

I’m building a business of things I cherish and want people to enjoy. I don’t collect because something is old. I collect because something deserves another life.

Some pieces are beautifully made. Some carry the quiet marks of time. Some simply stop me in my tracks. Together they form a modern cabinet of curiosities: furniture, art, jewelry, vintage clothing, eyewear, coins, books, and remarkable objects gathered with care.

My hope is that each piece finds a home where it will be appreciated, lived with, and enjoyed for years to come. Because the best objects are not hidden away. They become part of everyday life.
