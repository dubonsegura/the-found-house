Our Philosophy

Collected with Intention.
Meant to Be Lived With.

The Found House began with a simple belief: the objects we choose to live with shape the feeling of a home.

I do not collect because something is old. I collect because something deserves another life.

Some pieces are beautifully made. Some carry the quiet marks of time. Some simply stop me in my tracks.

Every object you see here is something I would proudly welcome into my own home: furniture, art, vintage clothing, jewelry, eyewear, coins, books, and remarkable objects that do not fit neatly into a category but deserve to be discovered.

The Found House is my modern cabinet of curiosities. My hope is that these pieces continue their journey into homes where they will be appreciated, lived with, and enjoyed for years to come.

Because the best objects are not hidden away. They become part of everyday life.
