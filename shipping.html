Shipping

Prepared with care.

Shipping is part of the customer experience, not an afterthought.

Shipping standards

Each order is packed with care using materials appropriate for the item’s size, weight, value, and fragility. Shipments include appropriate insurance based on value.

Insurance

The Found House includes shipping insurance as part of its shipping standard and pricing strategy. Customers are not expected to separately arrange insurance for standard shipments.

One-of-a-kind items

Because many pieces are one of a kind, replacement may not be available if an item is lost or damaged in transit. In those situations, we work toward a fair resolution consistent with shipping insurance and applicable consumer protection laws.

Expedited shipping

Overnight or expedited shipping may be available by request when practical. Additional costs for faster shipping are the customer’s responsibility unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Signature confirmation

Higher-value shipments may require signature confirmation for the protection of both the customer and The Found House.
