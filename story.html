Every Piece Has a Story

Stories worth continuing.

The Found House experience is built around discovery, research, and honest presentation.

Found House Story Cards

Whenever possible, a qualifying piece receives a Story Card: a short, thoughtful record of what we have learned about its style, maker, material, technique, condition, design appeal, or known history.

Some stories are richly documented. Others remain partially unknown. We believe both deserve honest presentation.

Included when appropriate

Story Card

Readable notes designed to make ownership more meaningful without exaggerating what is known.

Story & Collection Record

The Story & Collection Record is a Found House document that may include catalog details, dimensions, condition notes, research observations, and why the piece was selected for the collection.

It is a record of what is known and observed at the time the piece enters The Found House collection. It is not an independent appraisal or unsupported authentication.
