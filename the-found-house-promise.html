Our Promise

The Found House Promise

Everything we offer reflects the standards we hold ourselves to.

Honest Descriptions

Every piece is represented as accurately as possible. If an item has wear, repairs, or imperfections, we'll tell you. Character deserves to be celebrated—not hidden.

Original Photography

Every photograph is our own. We show each piece from multiple angles so you can appreciate its beauty, understand its condition, and shop with confidence.

Thoughtful Curation

Every item earns its place because of its craftsmanship, character, quality, or story.

Fair Representation

What you see is what you'll receive. We believe trust is earned through transparency.

Respect for Craftsmanship

We honor the work that went into creating each piece while preserving the details that make it unique.

Our Commitment

We believe remarkable things deserve remarkable homes.

Every discovery we share is chosen with care, photographed with honesty, and presented with respect.

Miguel
Founder, Found House Company
