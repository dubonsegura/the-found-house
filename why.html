Why The Found House Exists

Every remarkable piece deserves a remarkable home.

Beautiful things deserve to be discovered, appreciated, and enjoyed again.

The Found House grew from a love of discovering objects with character and the belief that thoughtful design can be found in unexpected places.

Some pieces are handmade. Others are vintage. Some are contemporary. Some are simply overlooked. What connects them is their craftsmanship, presence, and the feeling they bring to a space.

We carefully search for pieces with lasting beauty and thoughtfully curate a collection for every home that appreciates beautiful things. Along the way, we preserve what we learn so each story can continue with the next owner.

Our philosophy

  • We do not chase trends. We search for quality.
  • We do not invent stories. We document what we know.
  • We do not fill rooms with more. We look for pieces that matter.
  • We believe a home should hold objects that bring warmth, curiosity, and meaning.
  • We believe remarkable things deserve remarkable homes.

A welcoming collection

Not exclusive. Intentional.

The Found House is for homeowners, collectors, designers, gift buyers, and anyone who appreciates beautiful things. The goal is not to make a home look expensive. The goal is to help it feel considered, personal, and alive.
