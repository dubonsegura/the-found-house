Why The Found House Exists

Helping remarkable pieces find people who appreciate them.

Every remarkable piece deserves another chapter.

Too many beautiful things are overlooked, not because they have lost value, but because they are waiting for the right person to discover them. The Found House was created to find those pieces, respect their craftsmanship, and present them with honesty and care.

We are not simply resellers. We are curators. We buy with intention, research with humility, photograph with honesty, and package with respect.
